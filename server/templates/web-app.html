Safety Center

Your safety is our top priority. Tripsbnb provides tools, guidelines, and protections for every interaction on the platform.

Communication Protection

All chat messages are automatically filtered to prevent sharing of personal contact details outside the platform.

  • Phone numbers are automatically hidden
  • Email addresses are blocked from messages
  • External app mentions (WhatsApp, Telegram, etc.) are filtered
  • Social media handles are masked
  • Street addresses and meeting locations are filtered
  • GPS coordinates are blocked
  • Creative evasion attempts (spelled numbers, spaced digits) are caught

Payment Security

All transactions are processed through Stripe, ensuring your financial data is protected.

  • PCI-compliant payment processing
  • No vendor has access to your card details
  • Transparent 12% service fee on all bookings
  • Refund protection for cancelled bookings

Companion Safety

Special protections are in place for companion interactions.

  • Safety acknowledgment required before connecting
  • Verified companion badges for identity-checked profiles
  • In-app chat keeps all communication on-platform
  • Report and block functionality
  • Contact details only revealed after confirmed booking

Travel Safety

Stay safe at your destination with built-in tools and resources.

  • AI-powered emergency info for any destination
  • Local emergency numbers at your fingertips
  • Cultural etiquette and safety advisories
  • Verified vendor ratings and reviews

Privacy Controls

You control what information is shared and when.

  • Vendor contact info is masked until booking is confirmed
  • Your personal details are never shared with other users
  • Profile visibility settings
  • Data encryption in transit and at rest

Reporting & Support

If something goes wrong, we are here for you.

  • Report inappropriate behavior directly in chat
  • 24/7 safety support for active bookings
  • Dispute resolution for booking issues
  • Emergency contact hotline
Emergency?

If you are in immediate danger, contact local emergency services first. Then reach out to Tripsbnb support via the in-app chat for assistance with your booking.
